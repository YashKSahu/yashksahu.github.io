The Robot

Attack:

  • Frontier blades can damage opponent robots while spinning
  • Ramps can topple competing robots
  • High-torque motors can push robots (up to 8 kg) outside the arena

Defense:

  • Wheels have an extra rubber layer for better traction
  • Wooden armor at the back for added protection
  • Electronics shielded with transparent acrylic sheets for safety and visual monitoring
